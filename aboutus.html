About Us

As parents we know how hard it is to find and trust a babysitter. When we were looking for a babysitter through other websites, we found them to be convulated. Other sites kept requiring us to sign-up in order to check out their service. Since we weren't able to find a simple site we felt comfortable with, we started our own safe and easy way to find a sitter. This is a one stop shop for finding the right babysitter for you. It's important to know who our babysitters are as well as who our clients are. Our babysitters background are thoroughly check ensuring that both parties have "Peace of Mind."
